Connect Your Google Ads Account

This application allows users to connect their own Google Ads accounts using their own OAuth2 credentials.

How it works:

  1. You provide your Google OAuth2 credentials (Client ID & Secret from your Google Cloud Console)
  2. You enter your Google Ads Manager ID (Customer ID)
  3. Authenticate with Google using your credentials
  4. Grant permissions to access your Google Ads data
  5. Access your campaigns and performance data

This app doesn't store your OAuth credentials permanently. You provide your own Google Client ID and Secret, which are used only for your session. This ensures maximum security and control over your data.

What you'll need:

1. Google Cloud Console OAuth2 Credentials:

  • Google Client ID
  • Google Client Secret
  • Set redirect URI to: http://localhost:5000/callback

2. Your Google Ads Information:

  • Your Google Ads Customer ID (Manager Account ID)
  • Make sure MCM (Multi Customer Manager) is active
